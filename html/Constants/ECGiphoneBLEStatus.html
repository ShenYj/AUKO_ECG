ECGiphoneBLEStatus

@enum ECGiphoneBLEStatus

Represents the current state of a iPhone.

@constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusUnknown State unknown, update imminent. @constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusResetting The connection with the system service was momentarily lost, update imminent. @constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusUnsupported The platform doesn’t support the Bluetooth Low Energy Central/Client role. @constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusUnauthorized The application is not authorized to use the Bluetooth Low Energy role. @constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusPowerOff Bluetooth is currently powered off. @constant ECGiphoneBLEStatusPowerOn Bluetooth is currently powered on and available to use.
